Data Processing: Data are collected manually from the Yahoo finance website( https://finance.yahoo.com/sectors/), CompaniesMarketCap website(https://companiesmarketcap.com/) and Dow Jones financial dataset provided on KEATS. The data is taken for the years 2016 to 2018. Companies are grouped with respect to their sectors. Individual companies and sectors are analysed according to their earnings and market cap. Data aggregation and manipulation is performed using python.
